The retreat

Small by design,
generous by nature

Clove Beach was imagined as the opposite of the grand resort: an intimate collection of private villas where the team knows your name, your table and the way you take your morning tea.

Directly on the sand where the south coast begins, less than an hour from Colombo, it is close enough for a whim and far enough to feel like another world.
