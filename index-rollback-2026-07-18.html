Welcome to Clove

A quiet sanctuary,

Set along the golden shoreline of Wadduwa, Clove Beach is an intimate collection of private villas wrapped in tropical calm. Here, the days move gently, from tranquil swims to dishes that echo luxury.

Every detail is designed for refined comfort. A refreshing pool, a serene spa, an ocean-facing bar and lounge spaces that make each moment indulgent, and effortless.
